About Me

I am a Professional Land Surveyor based in California, with over 12 years of diverse experience spanning private land development, utilities, and transportation projects.

My journey began with union training through the Operating Engineers Local 3 apprenticeship program, and I have since expanded my expertise as a Part 107 certified SUAS pilot.

I am very passionate about continuous learning - I strive to master my craft while ensuring the journey remains exciting and fulfilling. Outside of work, I stay active through mountain biking, hiking, camping, and playing baseball.
